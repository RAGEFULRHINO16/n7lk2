THE_STORY // COLOMBO_DNA

Born in
Colombo.
Worn Everywhere.

N7 LK was founded on a single belief — that modest dressing is a canvas for extraordinary self-expression.

"We started in a small studio in Nugegoda with bolts of fabric, a dream, and an obsession with the perfect drape."

What began as a passion project between two sisters has grown into Sri Lanka's most-loved modest luxury label. We design in Colombo, source locally, and craft every garment with intention.
